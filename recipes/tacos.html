Some Great Tacos!

Description:

To offer tacos is such an obvious success! Many small bowls filled with such tasty ingredients - flavourfilled minced meat, diced vegetables, cool sour-cream, luxurious guacamole and even more! Let everyone around the table fill their tacos with their favorites. More tacoparty for everyone!

Ingredients:

  • Minced meat 500g
  • Taco spicemix 1 bag
  • Water 1dl

For serving:

  • Cherry Tomatoes 150g
  • One cucumber
  • Red onion (no skip this)
  • Corn 2dl
  • Shredded Cheese 150g
  • Sour Cream 3dl (not really)
  • Tomato Salsa (naaah)
  • Tortilla bread, 8 pieces
  • Nacho chips
  • Extra spices

Directions:

  1. Fry the minced meat in butter and rapeseed oil until it is crumbly and cooked through. Add taco seasoning mix and water. Simmer until the water has boiled. Remove from heat.
  2. Serve with all the accessories laid out in bowls. Feel free to fry the corn in a little butter and rapeseed oil until nicely colored.
  3. Done! :)
